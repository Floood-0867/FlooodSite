Workflow

Upon commissioning me, you'll be added into my queue. I'll inform you when I start working on the work, and the average turnaround once I start work is one day, however this is subject to change. For a retexture, I will use substance painter. For edits, assets, and clothing, I will use blender and send updates while I work. For all commissions, I will send updates and make any changes that are asked. I'll then procced into unity and set up the avatar's model, textures, and materials on the base model, as well as the assets or clothing. You'll then be sent the finished work as a .unitypackage with everything included to upload to your account.
